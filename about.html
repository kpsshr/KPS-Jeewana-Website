Welcome to KPS Jeewana

This School is a Part and Branch of Krishna public school(KPS) is "Affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi". Krishna public school set up in a sprawling lush green campus of about 12 acres in Sindhari Balotra of Rajasthan to provide futuristic world class education is an exclusively English medium residential public school which nurtures the talents of young boys and girls with the blending of text and technology to produce future citizens of harmonious personality. The school is being run with the commitment to make an indelible mark in the field of education by creating a global study centre of excellence. The school provides target oriented value based education up to middle level reflecting the latest advances in the field of school education equipped with the state of arts facilities, excellent infrastructure, highly qualified and experienced faculty, learning oriented and stimulating academic environment. K.P.S. is ideal for intellectual pursuits, innovation and research. The school helps its students to observe and understand the world in its enormous vibrancy. Complexity and splendor and equips them with the ability to examine and understand the relationships between various facts, principles and theories.
